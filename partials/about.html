Jackson and I love designing in the browser. A lot. But we want to love it more. As css authors and designers, when we step into a codebase, we have a lot of questions. And they can be time consuming to get answers to. We don't believe that should be the case because it's 2013 and we have computers.

This project is a start for us to play around with visualizing all of our questions.

We would love help!
We want your thoughts, your questions, your criticisms, and of course your pull requests :)

The data we are visualizing here is from some sites we were interested in looking at for various reasons. We'll be adding more data frequently, but would love to be allow for arbitrary css files / urls to drive generating the data.

While this isn't a complete tool by any means, we're excited about the start.
